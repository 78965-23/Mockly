Refund Policy

Last revised: June 2026

We want you to be satisfied with your Mockly subscription. If you are not completely happy, we offer a straightforward refund policy as outlined below.

1. Free Trial

Many features remain free forever. Paid plans may start with a 7‑day free trial. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be charged.

2. Money‑Back Guarantee

If you purchase a monthly or annual plan and are not satisfied, you can request a full refund within 14 days of the initial payment. After 14 days, refunds are not available for that billing period.

3. How to Request a Refund

Send an email to support@mockly.example.com with your account email, order reference, and the reason for the refund. We will process eligible requests within 5‑7 business days.

4. Exceptions

Refunds are not granted for partial usage, discounts, or promotional offers unless explicitly stated. Accounts terminated for violating our Terms & Conditions are not eligible for refunds.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.
